Combating Domestic Violence

Domestic violence is a serious issue, and everyone has the right to live a safe and dignified life. If you have become a victim of domestic violence or have witnessed it, you should immediately seek help. Here are some important tips:

  • Do not hesitate to ask for help. There are hotlines and organizations that provide support.
  • Domestic violence can be physical, psychological, or economic. All forms of violence are unacceptable.
  • If you feel in danger, call the emergency hotline or contact the police.
  • Keep any evidence of violence, such as photos or testimonies, as this may help in legal proceedings.
  • Remember, violence in any form is a crime, and you have the right to protection.

If you or someone you know is experiencing domestic violence, do not hesitate to ask for help. Your safety is important!
