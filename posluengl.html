Our Services

The patrol police provide a wide range of services to citizens. Here are some of the main services we offer:

  • Public order maintenance: Street patrols, ensuring safety at public events, law enforcement control.
  • Traffic regulation: Monitoring compliance with traffic regulations, assisting in road accidents, checking vehicle technical conditions.
  • Crime prevention: Detecting and preventing crimes, monitoring illegal activities.
  • Legal consultations: Providing legal advice to citizens, assisting in conflicts and legal violations.
  • Emergency response: Quick response to emergency situations, including fires, accidents, and natural disasters.
  • Investigation and search: Collecting evidence, investigating criminal cases, and searching for missing persons.

If you require any of these services, please contact the nearest police department or call the emergency hotline.
