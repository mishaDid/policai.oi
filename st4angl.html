Legislative Terminology

Legal terms are fundamental for understanding and applying legal norms. Familiarizing yourself with the terms used in legislation helps to navigate the legal field of Ukraine more effectively. Here are some key terms:

  • Law

    An official act adopted by the legislative body, which has binding force throughout the country.

  • Constitution

    The fundamental law of the state, defining the principles of government, rights, and duties of citizens.

  • Law

    A system of binding rules of conduct established by the state to ensure order in society.

  • Jurisdiction

    The authority of a government body to exercise its competence within a specific territory or over certain individuals.

  • Code

    A collection of laws that regulate a particular area of legal relations (e.g., criminal code, civil code).

Understanding legislative terminology helps to grasp the foundations of the legal system and ensures the correct application of legal norms in everyday life.
